THE LORE

In the year 2142, the digital realm is collapsing. You are a Runner, a program designed to traverse the corrupted sectors of the Mainframe.

Red glitches block your path, representing corrupted data clusters. Golden fragments represent lost memories. Your objective is simple: run as far as you can before the system purges you.
